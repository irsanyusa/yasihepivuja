How 15 Actresses That Started Their Careers as Kids Have Changed Over the Years

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 19, 2024

Many Hollywood stars started their careers in childhood. For example, the movie Léon paved the way for Natalie Portman to the world of big cinema, and Interview with the Vampire made 12-year-old Kirsten Dunst a big star. We wanted to discover how the actresses we remember as little girls have changed over the years. And it’s easy to track because they’ve left a trail of many movies in their wake.
