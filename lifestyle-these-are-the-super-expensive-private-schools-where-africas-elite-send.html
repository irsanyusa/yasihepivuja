These are the super expensive private schools where Africas elite send their kids

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, June 21, 2024

This means the presence of some of the richest people in the world and their families. These are the top schools in Africa’s major cities who cater to the children of the high and mighty.

Africa Leadership Academy, South Africa -$30,000

This private high school considers itself a Pan-African institution and runs an innovative curriculum, which merges academics with  courses in Entrepreneurial Leadership and African Studies

International School of Kenya - $26,000

This Accra-based school prides itself on its ability to send its graduates to some of the best schools of higher education around the world.
