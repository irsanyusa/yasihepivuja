America’s Got Talent Season 12 winner Darci Lynne Farmer recently hard launched her relationship on Instagram, sharing a cute picture with her boyfriend. She further confirmed the romance in a video with her fellow social media stars.

Darci Lynne Farmer Hard Launches New Boyfriend on Instagram

Earlier this week, Farmer shared a series of photos on Instagram from her time at the Calf Fry Festival in her home stage of Oklahoma. In one shot, she can be seen embracing a young man in a cowboy hat. In another picture, she kisses him on the cheek.

Based on the account tagged in the post, his name is Kendon Reed. However, his Instagram account is private, so not much more is known about him aside from that. Of course, people in the comments had plenty to say about the photos.

“OKAY HARD LAUNCH,” wrote one user. Fellow AGT Season 12 star Preacher Lawson wrote, “Tell him to get you home early or Daddy is going to kill him.” This is a reference to Farmer’s debut single “Push Our Luck.”

Farmer further confirmed that she’s in a relationship in a video collaboration with dancer Merrick Hanna and actress Gabrielle Nevaeh. The clip is captioned, “Dance if you’re single.” While Nevaeh enthusiastically dances, Farmer and Hanna stand still.

Farmer Is Branching Out with Music

Farmer won AGT with her ventriloquism act, but she has recently been branching out to sing without her puppets. Recently, she released her debut single “Push Our Luck.” She’s also shared snippets of other original songs on social media.

Earlier this year, Farmer competed on AGT: Fantasy League. In her first performance, she stepped away from puppet Petunia to perform on the guitar. In her later performance, she performed entirely without her puppets, singing “Push Our Luck.” Unfortunately, she didn’t make it to the next round.
