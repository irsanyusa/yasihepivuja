Gwen Stefani Announced as The Next IPSY Icon Box Curator

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 12, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by: Casey Durkin/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Gwen Stefani has announced an exciting new collaboration that’s perfect for her longtime beauty fans. The singer is curating a limited-edition IPSY Icon Box filled with her favorite makeup must-haves.

Gwen Stefani Announces Limited-Edition IPSY Icon Box

Stefani shared the news in an Instagram video where she modeled a glam pink dress. The collaboration is a natural fit for Stefani, who has always loved makeup and self-expression.

“Before there was music, there was makeup,” she shared. “As a little girl, I used to spend hours watching old Hollywood glam and all things vintage.”

Surprisingly, Stefani actually got her start working at a makeup counter in a department store. Now, Stefani is releasing her own brand, GXVE Beauty, and curating the IPSY box.

“I feel like my whole life has built to this moment,” she said. “I’m really proud of what we have developed, and I can’t wait to see the looks the IPSY community creates.”

IPSY gave a sneak peek of some of the products included in Stefani’s Icon Box. It features beauty essentials and long-time favorites such as a velvety matte lip color, a blendable bronzer stick, a hydrating medium-coverage foundation, and an antioxidant-rich salve for dry skin. There will be four more surprise products inside the box as well. Fans can also personalize by choosing three of the eight full-size items.

How to Get Gwen Stefani’s Icon Box?

The Icon Box is available as a quarterly upgrade to IPSY Glam Bag and BoxyCharm subscriptions. Existing members can visit the Icon Box tab in their account and click “Upgrade Your Box.”

New members can take IPSY’s Beauty Quiz to get matched with products suited to their needs and preferences. After signing up for Glam Bag or BoxyCharm, they can then upgrade to the Icon Box.

Stefani’s curated box will ship out in February, but quantities are limited. Fans should follow IPSY on Instagram for more product reveals coming soon.

While Stefani’s style has changed over the years, her beauty look often features bold red lips and retro-glam vibes. Now fans can recreate her iconic look themselves with the products she hand-picked.

The No Doubt frontwoman has always loved experimenting with makeup for self-expression. She did her own makeup for many of the band’s early tours.
