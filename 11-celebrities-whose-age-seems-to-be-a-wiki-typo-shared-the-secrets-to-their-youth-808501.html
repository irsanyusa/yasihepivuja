11 Celebrities, Whose Age Seems to Be a Wiki Typo, Shared the Secrets to Their Youth

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, September 3, 2024

It’s hard to argue with the fact that stars look stunning on red carpets. And of course, many of them look much younger than they really are. Sometimes, once we learn their date of birth, we simply can’t believe it!

We at Bright Side decided to look at the biographies of celebrities, did a good online search, and now, we’re going to tell you about the secrets to their fountain of youth.

Vera Wang (73 years old)

Vera Wang is 73, but she looks like she is 45 years old at the most. In many interviews, everyone asks her what her secret is. She has nothing to hide and admits that sleep is one of the main secrets. She also played a lot of sports in her youth.

Salma Hayek (55 years old)

Salma has a perfect body and perfect skin. In her blog, she says that one of her secrets is an orange cocktail that includes the fruits, turmeric, black pepper, and sea buckthorn.

Another secret is washing her face correctly. She doesn’t use any cleaners in the morning. She thinks that if you cleanse your face well before you go to sleep, the skin doesn’t need any rough products, and water is more than enough in the morning.

Halle Berry (55 years old)

lizabeth Goodenough / Everett Collection / East News

At the age of 55, Halle actively exercises, motivating women all around the world to follow her example. She posts her workouts on social media and spends a lot of time caring about her face.

One of her secrets is collagen masks. Halle also drinks bone broth, “You can make it, you can go to the butcher and get all the bones they’re going to throw away, and he’ll give them to you for free.”

Nicole Kidman (55 years old)

Elizabeth Goodenough / Everett Collection / East News

It is obvious that the actress has relied on plastic surgery and cosmetologists. But she also spends a lot of time supporting her youth and beauty. She regularly works out, eats only farm-fresh foods, and meditates 20 minutes a day.

The actress does hair masks and takes vitamins to make her nails stronger. Nicole also takes baths with a lot of moisturizing oil. Another life hack: she uses cleansing wipes to remove makeup after a long work day. And in her ordinary life, she never uses makeup and never forgets to use sunscreen.

Jennifer Aniston (53 years old)

The actress says that she’s surprised by the comments from people saying that she looks good for her age. She doesn’t think that, with age, people look worse. But she works a lot on her appearance. She starts every morning with a glass of celery juice and drinks diluted apple vinegar. She then has breakfast 30 minutes later.

There’s also a recipe for the famous salad that she had almost every day for 10 seasons of Friends. It’s made from bulgur, cucumbers, parsley, pistachios, and feta cheese. The actress also said that she’s not afraid of bread and eats it in moderation. She also takes collagen and works out a lot.

Julianne Moore (61 years old)

ANGELA WEISS / AFP / East News

Just like Nicole Kidman, Julianne doesn’t use makeup in her everyday life. Besides, she’s been using sunscreen on her face every day since the age of 23. She tries to only walk in the shadows. Before important appearances, Moore doesn’t eat sushi or other foods that are rich in sodium.

The actress is a fan of microneedling. Beauty experts use this procedure to stimulate the production of collagen and elastin. And of course, she works out, for example, she does yoga 4-5 times a week.

Sofía Vergara (50 years old)

Invision / Invision / East News

Sofia Vergara has a beautiful body, and she said that she doesn’t like working out, even though she has to do it. She works out 2-3 hours a week, and prefers water sports, yoga, and dancing. One of her secrets is using lipstick, since it makes the face look neat. She eats often — 5 times a day, and has no red meat at all.

Jennifer Garner (50 years old)

Invision / Invision / East News

Just like many of her colleagues, Jennifer protects her skin from the sun. She’s a fan of retinol, a beauty product that helps fight aging and skin imperfections. She uses cardio workouts to deal with stress. Jennifer doesn’t use a hairdryer: she lets her hair dry on its own.

Besides, her family and her grow their own fruits and vegetables, so she’s careful about what she eats.

Jennifer Lopez (53 years old)

In one of her interviews, Jennifer Lopez said that she doesn’t feel older than 20. She said that her secret is taking good care of her body and soul. She loves meditation, works out regularly, and tries different exercises. She says, “Sometimes I’m running, riding a bike, on an elliptical, or lifting weights. Other times I’m dancing in a studio for 8 hours a day. I just keep moving.”

Jennifer also shared the secret of her perfect skin. She calls it the 5 S’s: sleep, sunscreen, serums, supplements, and living a healthy, sane life.

Penélope Cruz (48 years old)

CJ Rivera / Everett Collection / East News

The actress said that she feels way more confident now than when she was young. The star has non-fat protein for breakfast, whole wheat foods, lots of fresh avocados, and olive oil. She has nuts and fruits for snacks. Penélope avoids gluten and drinks a lot of water.

Before she had her first child, she often worked out in the gym. Now, she prefers Bikram yoga, a system of hot yoga. She has no bad habits, and tries to avoid sugar and dairy products.

Angela Bassett (63 years old)

Invision / Invision / East News

The actress is at peace with her age and says that 60 is the new 40, and 70 is the new 50. She recommends having less stress. She uses different beauty products, all that matters is that they are good for her skin type. 2 times a week, she works out with a coach, and she has her own dietary formula.

“Monday, Tuesday, I may eat carbs and fruit. Tuesday, Wednesday, protein, veggies—veggies every day—no fats those 4 days, and then the last 3 days, I flood it with fat,” she said. “Good fat, though: maybe coconut oil, salmon, almonds, almond butter, you know, olive oil, that sort of thing.”

Do you have any life hacks for preserving your youth?
