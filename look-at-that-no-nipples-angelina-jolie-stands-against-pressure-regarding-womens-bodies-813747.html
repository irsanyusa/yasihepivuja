Look at That. No Nipples! Angelina Jolie Stands Against Pressure Regarding Womens Bodies

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 11, 2024

Recently Angelina Jolie, 48, opened up about certain instances when she found herself being self-critical of her own appearance. Notably, one such moment arose when she saw herself featured on posters, donning the iconic Lara Croft costume. This reflection left her astonished at the way her body had been portrayed as the ultimate action hero.

With a raw and honest perspective, Angelina Jolie’s experience sheds light on the pressures faced by individuals in the entertainment industry. Despite her global fame, she too encountered insecurities regarding her physical appearance.

Paramount/Courtesy Everett Collection/East News

Throughout the production of the Tomb Raider movies, Jolie underwent extensive physical and mental training to embody the role of an action star. The rigorous regimen allowed her to sculpt her physique, ensuring an authentic and captivating performance.

“They had me up at seven each morning and handed me a protein shake. Suddenly, a nutritionist was giving me five meals a day. But you had to do these things to maintain your energy,” the actress comments about those times.

SAUL LOEB/AFP/East News, SAUL LOEB/AFP/East News

Even with her impeccable physical condition for both Tomb Raider films, Angelina Jolie faced challenges when it came to the portrayal of her physique in film posters. Shockingly, certain images were subjected to edits intended to enhance her appearance, which Jolie openly expressed her offense towards.

“I still can’t get over that they airbrushed my nipples out of the posters for Lara. Look at that. No nipples, I think it’s rather strange and odd. Nipples are lovely.”

STEFANI REYNOLDS/AFP/East News

While Angelina Jolie has been widely regarded as one of Hollywood’s most alluring stars, her perception of herself differed from how others saw her. In a revealing interview, the renowned actress confessed to initially feeling that her looks were somewhat unconventional. However, as she matured, Jolie learned to appreciate her distinctiveness, realizing that her unique features were a reflection of her lineage and a resemblance to her beloved mother.

Follow us as we delve into Jolie’s powerful perspective, shedding light on her unwavering commitment to challenging norms and advocating for body positivity. Discover her inspiring efforts to promote acceptance, equality, and a more inclusive representation of women in the public sphere.

Angelina Jolie’s parenting approach is both unique and inspiring. Prioritizing freedom in education and self-expression, she encourages her six children to carve their own paths. While she maintains certain boundaries to shield them from the pitfalls of fame, her overarching philosophy is rooted in understanding and open communication, ensuring her children grow up in an environment that celebrates individuality and resilience.

Fashion enthusiasts have much to learn from Angelina Jolie’s impeccable style. From her preference for nude pumps that elongate her legs to her penchant for black, white, and pastel ensembles, Jolie’s wardrobe choices exude elegance. Her mastery of power dressing, signature makeup looks, and strategic use of jewelry further accentuate her natural beauty. Whether gracing the red carpet or attending a business event, Jolie’s “Greek goddess” silhouette and minimalist accessories consistently turn heads.

In a rare and heartfelt social media post, Angelina Jolie paid tribute to her late mother, Marcheline Bertrand, on what would have been her 73rd birthday. Sharing a vintage photo of them together, Jolie reflected on her mother’s battle with cancer and her own preventive surgeries. The post not only celebrated her mother’s memory but also emphasized the importance of regular health check-ups, especially for those with a family history of cancer.
