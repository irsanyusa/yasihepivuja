Natural Hair Girl: Natural hairstyles to make in under an hour

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, July 18, 2024

You can do these styles in under an hour:

1. Leave your full afro

This is an overlooked way to style your natural hair, but there is nothing wrong with letting your afro be.

Before you do this, make sure your hair is properly hydrated. Use some conditioner, coconut, argan, jojoba, or olive oil to give your hair the required sheen.

ADVERTISEMENT

Also, trim split ends.

2. Leave your twists out

Making twists outs and loosening it, gives your hair a curly look. Condition your hair properly before you twist it.

This look is better done overnight but even an hour before you go out can give it a semi-curly look.

3. Make two big cornrows

ADVERTISEMENT

There are so many options for two big cornrows. If your hair is not long enough, you can always add extensions. If it is long, you can either leave it as it is or roll it up.

You will need a copious amount of styling gel to smoothen your hair out.

4. Two puffs

This is an easy way to pack short hair. Just partition your hair into two puffs.

ADVERTISEMENT

5. Two braids in front

You can just make little braids like telephone wires and pack the rest of your hair.

Having a good styling gel and edge control is important to getting the desired looks.
