Outfit of the day- Millen Magese

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, July 21, 2024

OOTD inspiration is a beautiful yellow dress worn by international supermodel, Millen Magese, who is currently in Nigeria on business and pleasure.

The super stylish model looked beautiful in a flowery lace dress, that was mostly yellow with various other colors added on the edges. The dress by Tiannah’s Place was accessorized with Valentino shoes and a bag by Okiki Marinho.
