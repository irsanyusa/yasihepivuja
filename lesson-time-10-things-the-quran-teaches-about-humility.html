10 things the Quran teaches about humility

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 30, 2024

Here are 10 Quran verses that talks about humility.

1. Surah Al-Furqan 25:63 - The servants of the Most Merciful are those who walk upon the earth in humility, and when the ignorant address them, they say words of peace.

2. Surah Al-A’raf 7:55 - Call upon your Lord with humility and in private. Verily, He does not love transgressors.

3. Surah Al-A’raf 7:205 - Remember your Lord in yourselves with humility and in private without announcing it in the mornings and evenings, and do not be among the heedless.

4. Surah Al-Isra 17:24 - Lower to your parents the wing of humility out of mercy and say: My Lord, have mercy upon them as they brought me up when I was small.

5. Quran 23:02 - Successful indeed are the believers who humble themselves in their prayers.

6. Quran 31:18 -  And turn not your face away from men with pride, nor walk in insolence through the earth.  Verily, God likes not each arrogant boaster.

7 Quran 25:63 - And the slaves of God are those who walk on the earth in humility and calmness, and when the foolish address them (with bad words) they reply back with mild words of gentleness.

8. Quran 6:42 - And We have already sent [messengers] to nations before you, [O Muhammad]; then We seized them with poverty and hardship that perhaps they might humble themselves.

9. Quran 22:34 - Give good news to the humble-hearted.

10. Quran 11:23 - Indeed, they who have believed and done righteous deeds and humbled themselves to their Lord – those are the companions of Paradise; they will abide eternally therein.
