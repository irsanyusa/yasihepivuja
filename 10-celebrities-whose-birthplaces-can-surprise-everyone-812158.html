10+ Celebrities Whose Birthplaces Can Surprise Everyone

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 18, 2024

Since 1970, the number of people who left their birth country to live elsewhere has tripled. Among those are stars from the world of cinema and TV that we know well. And more often than not, you could never guess the places they were born.

Invision/Invision/East News

Emma Watson is British but she was actually born in Paris, France.

Evan Agostini/Invision/East News

Amy Adams was born in Vicenza, Italy, and only settled with her family in the United States when she was 8 years old.

VALERIE MACON/AFP/East News

Joaquin Phoenix was born in Río Piedras, Puerto Rico, and went to live in Los Angeles at 5 years old with his family.

Invision/Invision/East News

Charlize Theron’s ancestry includes Dutch, French, and German roots, but she was actually born in Benoni, South Africa.

Laurent VU/SIPA/SIPA/East News

Sebastian Stan was born in Constanța, Romania, but he also lived in Austria and the United States during his childhood.

Collin Xavier/Image Press Agency ABACA/Abaca/East News

Lupita Nyong’o was born in Mexico City, Mexico, and raised in Kenya from the age of 3.

Scott Garfitt/Invision/East News

Bruce Willis was born in Idar-Oberstein, West Germany, in 1955. 2 years later, his father relocated the family to New Jersey in the US.

zz/John Nacion/STAR MAX/IPx/Associated Press/East News

Hugo Weaving, who famously played Agent Smith in The Matrix films, was born in Ibadan, British Nigeria.

Hahn Lionel/ABACA/Abaca/East News

Millie Bobby Brown was born to British parents in Marbella, Spain.

Invision/Invision/East News

Nina Dobrev was born in Sofia, Bulgaria, but moved to Canada at a young age.

Fati Sadou/ABACAPRESS.COM/Abaca/East News

Keanu Reeves was born in Beirut, Lebanon, but was raised in Toronto, Canada.

Young Chris/CP/ABACA/Abaca/East News
