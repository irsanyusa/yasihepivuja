Ancient Egyptian names and their meanings

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, May 19, 2024

Whether it is the ancient pyramids or the Nile, there is always something spectacular about Egypt as a country. Similarly, Egyptian names are a magical blend of the country's historical past and present.

In ancient communities, people chose names with a lot of care. They served to bring credit on the personality of an individual or depict their parents' devotion to a particular god or place. In some instances, some were a reflection of the time when the child was born. Ancient Egyptian names had cultural and religious significance because Egyptians believed the names symbolized life, beauty and connection with gods

Egyptian names

One crucial factor to consider before you settle on any given name for your son or daughter is the meaning behind it. Usually, adjectives or nouns are prevalent whenever one is being identified. Individuals can be identified with objects like trees, animals, for example, a lion or by descriptions of events and characters.
