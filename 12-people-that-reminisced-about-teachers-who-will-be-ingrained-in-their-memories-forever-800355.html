12 People That Reminisced About Teachers Who Will Be Ingrained in Their Memories Forever

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, August 9, 2024

My 3rd grade teacher was the best! During the book fair, where you could go to the library and buy books to keep. One of our classmates really wanted a certain book, but his parents were going through a hard time financially. Our amazing teacher gave him $10-$20 to go get the book! After the student bought it, there was change, which he tried to give back to the teacher, but my teacher refused to accept it! BEST. TEACHER. EVER. God bless him.
