PSquare, Chidinma Sing Birthday Song For Beat 99.9FM

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, July 14, 2024

September 9, 2014 4:29 PM

The birthday song was led by Ms. Kedike and she received back up from Peter and Paul Okoye as well as Olisa.

The Beat 99.9FM marks its five year anniversary today September 9 and guess who dropped by to sing a birthday song for the renown radio station?

Chidinma and the PSquare brothers stopped by the radio for a chat with Olisa Adebua, Maria and O.C but couldn't leave the radio without doing what they know how to do best - Sing!
