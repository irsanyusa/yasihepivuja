Robert Pattinson, Suki Waterhouse Already Listing Possible Names for Their First Baby

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, June 18, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Entertainment Celebrity

Photo by Alberto E. Rodriguez/Getty Images

Reading Time: 3 minutes

Robert Pattinson and Suki Waterhouse certainly can’t wait to meet their little one. The couple are already listing possible names of their baby, but they haven’t fully chosen yet.

Robert Pattinson, Suki Waterhouse Excited for the Arrival of Their Baby

A source from Us Weekly said that Pattinson and Waterhouse have excitedly been gearing up for their future as parents. As they wait for the arrival of their baby, the couple have been busying themselves with setting up a nursery.

“They’ve started working on the nursery, the crib, tons of clothes, bottles and a bunch of other baby supplies,” a source said. “They’ve also begun coming up with a list of names but haven’t decided on anything specifically.”

At present, the couple are “all up for the décor for the nursery,” and they’re not doing it all by themselves. Waterhouse’s family is part of the nursery construction team.

“Suki is very close with her siblings, and they are all thrilled for her and Rob while her sisters have even helped in some of the preparing,” the insider said. “It’s a very happy time for the whole family, and they can’t wait to meet the little one.”

Waterhouse confirmed her pregnancy in November 2023 during her performance at the Corona Capital 2023 Music Festival in Mexico City.

“I thought I’d wear something sparkly today because I thought it might distract you from something else that’s going on,” she said, pointing to her baby bump. “I’m not sure if it’s working.”

Waterhouse and Pattinson have been dating since July 2018. In 2023, the couple moved in together thinking it was “finally the right time to expand their family.” According to another insider, the pair hid the pregnancy from their friends before they revealed the thrilling news.

She Had Her Emmys Dress Redesigned Due to Her Baby Bump

Amid her pregnancy, Waterhouse hit the red carpet at the 2023 Emmy Awards. She attended the event as Daisy Jones & The Six is nominated for Outstanding Limited Series or Anthology Series. In an interview with E!, she stated that her outfit had to be reworked so her baby bump would not be compromised.

“I was really excited about this look,” she said. “I tried it on a couple months ago but… the fitting was just me holding it to my body. So, they had to really take it apart and had to redesign this dress to make it fit the bump. But it does and I’m really happy that it worked.”

Waterhouse and Pattinson are not only looking forward for their baby. Multiple news outlets reported last year that the two are planning to tie the knot.

“I’m shocked that I’m so happy with someone for nearly five years,” Waterhouse previously said.
